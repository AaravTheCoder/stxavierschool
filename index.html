What's New

  • New School Food Dispensers

    Now students can use dispensers to get there food. The dispenser machine is set to our daily routine. Sometimes, the machines might break, so we still have our school chefs. We also are still telling the chefs to make the meals for better so they are adding more ingredients to the recipe.



  • New School Attendance Message System

    When students come to the school, their parents get a notification that your child has arrived. We have done that so you know that your child has came to school safely.


  • New School Fire Drill System

    Since there are so many fires going around, every twice a month, we are making students do fire drills. We want to make the students know what to do when there is fire. Remember; Our first rule is to keep your child safe.
