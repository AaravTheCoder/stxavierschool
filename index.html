Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
who won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

Max Eswell won the debate on the pollution. He told everyone that we should not use coal so much and start bicycling and other stuff. He also said that people are trying to make solar power cars so we could stop pollution. He also said that people should make factories solar power based. He won a medal.

who won first prize at the inter school football competition

who won first prize in the inter school science project competition

Zoe Artap won the 60th Science Competition. She made the project about how plants can grow on Mars! Wow! It was hard work, she said, but she finished it. The teachers there were really impressed with the project and she won a medal prize
